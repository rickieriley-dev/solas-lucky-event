How it works

Spin the Lucky Wheel to win coins. Every spin uses one chance.

  • You earn 1 free chance every 2 hours, automatically — no need to do anything.
  • Chances can be saved up to a max of 5 at a time.
  • Once you're at the max, the timer pauses until you spend a chance.
  • Landing on a coin prize adds that amount straight to your balance.
  • "Try Again" means no coins were won that spin.
